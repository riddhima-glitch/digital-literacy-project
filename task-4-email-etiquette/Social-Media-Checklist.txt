Social Media Responsibility Checklist

Do's:

Enable Privacy Settings: Regularly review your account security and privacy settings to control who sees your content.

Fact-Check Before Sharing: Verify the authenticity of news or links before reposting them to avoid spreading misinformation.

Highlight Achievements: Use your platforms to share academic projects, certifications, or volunteer work to attract recruiters.

Network Professionally: Connect with alumni and industry leaders on platforms like LinkedIn to build your career path.

Don'ts:

Post Sensitive Info: Never share your home address, phone number, or government ID numbers publicly

Engage with Trolls: Avoid participating in online arguments or "flame wars" that can damage your digital reputation.

Post Offensive Content: Refrain from using hate speech, profanity, or discriminatory language in comments or posts.

Complain About Faculty/Company: Avoid venting about your university or future employers on public forums.

Share "In-the-Moment" Regrets: Never post while you are angry or highly emotional; always think before you click.
